Turbeculosis

Tuberculosis (TB) is a contagious bacterial infection caused by Mycobacterium tuberculosis, primarily affecting the lungs but potentially impacting other parts of the body. It spreads through airborne droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or speaks.
